Role Guide

Recommended defaults for the current app suite.

Rake Manager

Use `command-center.html`, scope by train or train-rake, read-write for orders and complaints.

Base Kitchen Manager

Use `base-kitchen.html`, scope by base kitchen ID, read-write for kitchen orders and indents.

Customer Care

Use `customer-care.html`, all or selected train scope, read-write for complaints and resolution notes.

Control Tower

Use `control-tower.html`, approve/checker rights for catalog, partners, riders and route master.
